About AurinkoLab Ry

Empowering the next generation of sustainable innovators through hands-on solar technology education.

Our Mission

AurinkoLab Ry is a Finnish non-profit organization dedicated to fostering innovation in sustainable technology through youth-led engineering projects. Our passionate team of teenage engineers designs, builds, and operates solar-powered go-karts while sharing their knowledge with the community.

Founded on the belief that young minds can drive the future of clean energy, we combine education, innovation, and fun to create meaningful learning experiences that inspire the next generation of environmental leaders.
